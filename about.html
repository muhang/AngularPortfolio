About Me

I am a 23 year old front-end developer and UX designer currently living in New York. After graduating with a BA in Philosophy from Bard College, I realized that the best way to implement what I've learned from my studies on experience is to create and design new user interfaces for everyone to interact with and enjoy. I am a self-taught programmer and developer, and refined my skills professionally in my time with Mark & Phil, where I worked as a creative technologist. I currently do freelance work in front-end development, microsite design, and WordPress development, although my true passion lies in newer web technologies, such as Ruby on Rails and AngularJS. I am always seeking new opportunities to grow and learn, and new ways to explore the intricacies of user experience and design.
